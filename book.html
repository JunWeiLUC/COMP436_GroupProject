The Big Brick House: Memories of a Family

Author: The Grandchildren of Joseph and Ethel Moonan

No place is a place until things that have happened in it are remembered in history, ballads, yarns, legends, or monuments.

- Wallace Stegner, The Sense of Place   

When word reached us in late-2009 that the big brick house in Waseca was for sale, we suddenly found ourselves immersed in an email conversation (often with a photo or two attached) about our childhood years, our grandparents, and our connections to the house. After the house was sold, in January 2011, to a family with six children, we decided to collect our e-mail-shared memories and photos into a book we could give to the new owners (and to ourselves): the story of the big Brick House as we knew it.

We wish the new family years and years of love-filled fun in the big brick house, and wonderful memories for a lifetime.
