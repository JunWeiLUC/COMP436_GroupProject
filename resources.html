Child's History of Waseca County

Child's History of Waseca County Minnesota: From Its First Settlement in 1854 to the Close of the Year 1904. A Record of Fifty Years. THE STORY OF THE PIONEERS was written by James Child in 1905. The book was republished around 1989, and now freely available from various websites in different online versions.

We have included as a resources because it offers users a chance to see the general history of Waseca during the time period leading up to when the Moonan house was built. The first half of the book is an account of of Waseca's first 50 years from James Child's journal. The second half is composed of short biographical sketches of many of the prominent citizens of the region. Child notes that John Moonan was in the process of building a fine house.
